# Interviews

## Introduction
Interviews were conducted with potential users to understand their workflow and challenges in managing documents and projects.

---

## Interview 1: Student User

### Questions Asked:
- How do you currently manage your study materials?
- What problems do you face with file organization?

### Answers:
- Uses Google Drive and notes apps separately
- Finds it difficult to organize files across platforms
- Often forgets where files are stored

---

## Interview 2: Developer

### Questions Asked:
- What tools do you use for project management?
- What limitations do you face?

### Answers:
- Uses GitHub, Notion, and Trello together
- Switching between tools is inefficient
- Needs unified workspace solution

---

## Key Insights
- Users prefer one centralized system
- File organization is a major pain point
- Simplicity and speed are important

---

## Conclusion
Interviews confirm the need for a unified workspace management system that combines documents, projects, and media in one platform.
