# Surveys

## Introduction
A small survey was conducted among students and potential users to understand their challenges in managing projects, documents, notes, and images.

---

## Survey Participants
- Total Participants: 10
- Students: 8
- Developers: 2

---

## Survey Questions and Results

### 1. Do you use multiple tools to manage your work?

| Response | Percentage |
|-----------|------------|
| Yes | 80% |
| No | 20% |

---

### 2. Do you find it difficult to organize files and documents?

| Response | Percentage |
|-----------|------------|
| Yes | 70% |
| No | 30% |

---

### 3. Would you prefer a single platform for managing projects, notes, documents, and images?

| Response | Percentage |
|-----------|------------|
| Yes | 90% |
| No | 10% |

---

### 4. Which feature is most important to you?

| Feature | Percentage |
|-----------|------------|
| Document Management | 35% |
| Project Tracking | 30% |
| File Storage | 20% |
| Search Functionality | 15% |

---

## Key Findings

- Most users rely on multiple tools.
- File organization is a common problem.
- Users prefer a centralized workspace solution.
- Document and project management are the highest-priority features.

---

## Conclusion

Survey results indicate strong interest in a unified workspace management platform. The findings support the development of the Smart Workspace Manager as a centralized system for managing projects, documents, notes, and images.
